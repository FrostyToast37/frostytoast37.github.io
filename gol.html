Conway's Game of Life

Instructions

-Click the cells to make them alive
-Once you have the cells that you want alive, press the start button to begin the simulation
-When you want to change something click the stop button, and then you can start again once you've made the changes

Rules

-Underpopulation: A live cell with fewer than two living neighbors dies in the next generation.
-Survival: A live cell with two or three living neighbors survives to the next generation.
-Overpopulation: A live cell with more than three living neighbors dies in the next generation.
-Reproduction: A dead cell with exactly three living neighbors becomes alive in the next generation.
